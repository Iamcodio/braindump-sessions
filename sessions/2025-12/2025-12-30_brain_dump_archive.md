### Safety Architecture (CRITICAL FOR MEDICAL DEVICE)

- Understands cultural context (Irish/British dark humor)

- Keyword detection for genuine crisis indicators
- Pattern matching for threat language

- Role: Final intervention authority

**Critical Principle:** Don't leave life-safety decisions to something that can be jailbroken

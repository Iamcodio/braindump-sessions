### Vitreous China Material Science

**Composition:**
- Glass oxides (silica-based) fuse during high-temp firing
- Creates non-porous, waterproof surface
- Metal oxides (iron, cobalt, chromium) = color + bonding
- Oxide network arrests crack propagation
- Compressive stress at oxide sites prevents fracture spreading
- Same principle: dental porcelain, glasses, ceramics

### Polymath Definition Discovery (Evening)
**Learned Today:** The word "dilettante" - opposite of polymath

**Definition Breakdown:**
- **Polymath:** Knowledge spans many subjects, draws on complex bodies of knowledge to solve problems
- **Specialist:** Depth but lacks breadth
- **Dilettante:** Superficial breadth, acquires skills "for their own sake without regard to understanding broader applications or implications and without integrating it"

**Key Insight:**
- **Renaissance Person:** Not just broad interests or superficial knowledge, but profound knowledge and proficiency/expertise in at least SOME fields
